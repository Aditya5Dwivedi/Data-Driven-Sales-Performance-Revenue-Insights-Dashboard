# 📊 Data-Driven Sales Performance & Revenue Insights Dashboard

**Python · SQL · Power BI · Excel**

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze business performance, sales trends, profit margins, regional insights, and product-level profitability.

It enables users to monitor **key sales KPIs, revenue trends, and market performance** through dynamic visualizations that update in real time using slicers and filters.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 📈 KPI Overview

The dashboard includes interactive KPI cards for:

* **Sales Projection**
* **Product Units**
* **KPI**
* **Returns**

These metrics dynamically update with selected filters.

---

### 🥧 Sales by Segment

Interactive pie chart showing sales contribution across:

* Consumer
* Corporate
* Home Office

---

### 🌍 Sales by Market

Visualizes sales distribution across global markets:

* Asia Pacific
* Europe
* US / Canada
* LATAM
* Africa

---

### 🗺️ Sales by Region

World map with bubble-based sales visualization for region-wise analysis.

---

### 👤 Profit by Customer

Bar chart showing top customers contributing maximum profit.

---

### 📦 Product Profitability

Includes:

* **Top 5 products by profit**
* **Bottom 5 products by profit**

Helps identify high-impact and low-performing products.

---
